Frequently Asked Questions

What is Doodle This?

Doodle This is a website where you can draw anything you like in a smooth and intuitive online sketchbook, get ideas for drawings from randomly generated prompts, save your best work in a free account, or buy prints to show off your artistic talents.

How do I use the sketchbook?

You can find the sketchbook on the sketchbook page. We've tried to make it as easy and intuitive as possible to use the sketchbook and all of the drawing tools, but if you'd like some help you can push the "Help" button and take a tour of all of the sketchbook's features.

I've made a mistake, can I undo it?

You can use the 'Undo' button to undo your last action on the sketchbook, but only your last action - you can't step back through every line you've drawn.

Why just one? We want the sketchbook to feel fun and natural, like drawing on a real page. But there's nothing fun about your hand slipping while you're working on some fine detail, or flooding half your canvas with the fill tool when you thought you'd clicked on the pencil, so we added an undo you can use to take back big mistakes.

Can I save my doodles?

Yes! You can save up to three doodles in your account. If you don't have an account, you can sign up here. When you've signed up or signed in, click the "Save / Load" button on the sketchbook page, then just pick a save slot, give your drawing a title if you'd like to, and then click "Save".

How do I load my doodles?

While signed in to your account, click the "Save / Load" button on the sketchbook page a to open the menu. Select which drawing you'd like to load, then click "Load" to load it onto your sketchbook. This will overwrite anything currently drawn on your sketchbook, so make sure you've saved anything you want to keep!

If you just want to admire your saved doodles without loading them onto your sketchbook, you can view them in the Gallery section of your Account page. Or, better yet, why not buy a print so you can have a permanent reminder of your artistic genius?

Can I delete my doodles?

Yes, you can delete your doodles from the Gallery section of your account page. Alternatively, you can simply save over any doodles that you don't want to keep with new artwork.

How do I place an order?

If you'd like to buy a print of some of your doodles, you can do that by visiting the Prints page and then clicking on the type of print you'd like to buy to see the options. Choose the size and/or colour of the product you'd like to get printed with your art, then choose the doodle you'd like to print on it. Finally, select the quantity and then click "Add to cart".

If you don't have an account, you can only print your current sketchbook's contents, but if you have an account, you can print any of your saved doodles, too.

Once you've added all the products you want to your shopping cart, click on the cart icon on the navigation bar or the 'Checkout Now' button in the message box to view your cart and make sure everything you want is in there. Then click "Checkout Now"and fill in your details to place your order.
